The Farm Story

Fazenda Capelavelha is a women-owned regenerative organic cacao farm dedicated to sustainable agriculture and empowering women in farming. This farm represents the vital role women play in building sustainable food systems and demonstrates how regenerative practices can create both environmental and social impact.

The farm specializes in unique cacao processing techniques, including the innovative caramelized cacao beans featured in AGL10. This special preparation brings out rich, caramel notes while preserving the natural essence of the cacao, showcasing the farm's commitment to both quality and innovation.
