The Farm Story

Paulo and his wife run La do Sitio, an award-winning cacao farm in the Amazon rainforest of Par√°, Brazil. La do Sitio has won multiple regional cacao awards, recognizing their commitment to quality and sustainable farming in the heart of the Amazon.

Located deep in the Amazon Rainforest, La do Sitio represents the best of regenerative agroforestry. The multiple regional cacao awards won by La do Sitio are a testament to their dedication to excellence. Each award recognizes not just the quality of their cacao, but also their commitment to regenerative practices that benefit both the land and the community. This farm stands as a model for how award-winning quality and environmental stewardship can go hand in hand.
